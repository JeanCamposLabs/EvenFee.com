1. What EvenFee does

EvenFee helps Amazon sellers recover overcharged FBA fulfillment fees caused by Amazon mis-measuring a product's dimensions or weight. With your authorization, we read a small set of your Amazon Selling Partner data, analyse it for fee overcharges, and prepare reimbursement claims for you to file.

2. Data we access (only with your authorization)

  • FBA Fee Preview — your product catalog's Amazon-recorded dimensions, weight, assigned size tier, and fulfillment fees.
  • Aggregated units sold — order-count metrics per product (Amazon Selling Partner API order metrics), used only to size the recoverable amount.

We do not access, collect, or process buyer / consumer personal data (PII). EvenFee never requests buyer names, addresses, contact details, or order-level customer information. We work only with your product, fee, and aggregate-sales data.

3. Data we store

  • Your Amazon authorization (refresh) token — held in a dedicated secrets manager (Google Secret Manager), never in our code or logs, and used only to read the data above.
  • Your selling-partner identifier and the audit results (the overcharge findings we computed) — in our database, so we can show and act on them.

4. How we use it

Solely to detect FBA fee overcharges and prepare your reimbursement claims. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or repurpose it for anything beyond this disclosed purpose.

5. Sub-processors

  • Google Cloud (Cloud Run, Secret Manager) — hosting and secret storage.
  • Neon — managed PostgreSQL database.

Data is hosted in the [EU region — confirm]. We add no other third parties without notice.

6. Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Authorization tokens kept in a secrets manager with least-privilege, scoped access.
  • Data minimization by design — we pull only the fields we need and never buyer PII.

7. Retention & your control

We retain your token and audit data only while you are an active client. You can revoke EvenFee's access at any time in Amazon Seller Central → Apps & Services → Manage Your Apps; on revocation or request, we delete your token and stored data. Request access or deletion at [support email].

8. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data, and to object to or restrict processing. Contact [support email]; you may also complain to your data protection authority.

9. Compliance

We handle Amazon data in line with the Amazon Acceptable Use Policy and Data Protection Policy, and with the GDPR.
